Hiring: Lead Organizer, New England VegFest

Seeking an emerging leader to play a leadership role in organizing the 6th annual New England VegFest

Background: New England VegFest is one of the largest vegetarian events in North America. Last year the festival attracted 10,000 attendees, 140+ exhibitors & 100+ volunteers. The 2016 event will be on Sunday, May 1 at the DCU Center in Worcester, Massachusetts.
